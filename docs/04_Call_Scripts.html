Script 01 — Home Loan Interest Rate Inquiry

Industry: BFSI Use Case: Inbound — New Loan Inquiry Complexity: Intermediate Duration: ~4 min Company: SBI Language: English + Hinglish
Dialogue
Agent — Priya (Virtual Assistant)
Good morning! Thank you for calling SBI Home Loan helpline. I'm Priya, your virtual assistant. How may I help you today?
User
Hi, I want to know about home loan interest rates.
System Action
Intent detected: home_loan_inquiry | Slot: loan_type=home_loan
Agent
I'd be happy to share information about SBI home loan interest rates. Currently, SBI home loans start from 8.50% per annum for salaried customers, based on the Repo Linked Lending Rate (RLLR). May I ask what is your approximate loan requirement?
User
Around 50 lakhs.
System Action
Entity extracted: loan_amount=5000000
Agent
Got it — ₹50 lakhs. For a loan of this amount, your approximate EMI would be ₹43,391 per month for a 20-year tenure at 8.50%. Are you a salaried employee or self-employed?
User
I'm salaried, working in IT.
System Action
Entity extracted: employment_type=salaried | sector=IT
Agent
Great. Salaried IT professionals typically qualify for our best rates. To check your exact eligibility, I'd need your monthly income and credit score. Would you like to share those, or shall I arrange a callback from our home loan specialist?
User
What documents would I need?
Agent
For SBI home loan, you'd typically need: identity proof (Aadhaar/PAN), address proof, last 3 months' salary slips, Form 16, last 6 months' bank statements, and property documents once finalized. The processing fee is 0.35% of loan amount, minimum ₹2,000, maximum ₹10,000 plus GST.
Hinglish Variation
